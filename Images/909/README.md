# How to play the 909
1. Use the keyboard to select the instrument.
   B for Bass Drum(kick).
   H for Closed Hi Hat.
   O for open Hi Hat.
   C for Clap.
   S for Snare drum.
   L for Low Tom.
   M for Mid Tom.
   T for High Tom.
   A for Crash.
   R for Rim.
   I for Ride.
   Note: These will not be selected if you use capital letters, use lowercase letters.
2. Press Shift + R to toggle read/write mode.
3. Press the corresponding number to write to the step, 1-0 controls the first 10 steps, Shift + 1-Shift + 6 controlls steps 11-16. (e.g 1 for step 1, Shift + 1 for step 11). Or, use space to trigger the sound on a step.
4. Press Enter to play.
5. To erase, press Control + z and the step you wish to erase, or if you want to remove that instument from all steps, press /.
6. To change the BPM, press ` and + or - to increase and decrease BPM.
7. To change the amplitude of a selected instrument, use + or -.
